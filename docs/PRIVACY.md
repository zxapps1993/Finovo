## The Short Version

- Finovo is **privacy-first**. The financial information you enter is **stored on your device**.
- There is **no Finovo server** that stores your financial records.
- **iCloud sync is optional and off by default.** If you turn it on, your data syncs through **your own private iCloud account** — we never receive a copy.
- Receipt scanning and text recognition happen **entirely on your device**.
- The **free tier shows ads via Google AdMob**, which involves limited data processing by Google. **Subscribing removes ads.**
- We do **not** sell your personal information for money.

## 1. Who We Are

Finovo is developed and operated by **Ghayor Abbas**, an individual developer, who acts as the data controller for the limited personal data described below. You can contact us at **zxapps1993@gmail.com**.

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Account information
If you choose **Sign in with Apple**, we store on your device an Apple-provided user identifier, and — only if you choose to share them on first sign-in — your name and email address (which may be Apple's private relay address). If you continue as a **Guest**, no account information is collected. This information is stored locally on your device (and in your private iCloud if you enable sync). We do not receive a copy on any server we control.

### b. Financial information you provide
Finovo stores what you enter or capture, which may include: expenses and receipts, income, merchant or source names, amounts and currencies, dates, categories, notes, payment-method labels, wallets and account balances, budgets, recurring bills and subscriptions, and bill-split details including names of people you add to a split.

### c. Receipt images and documents
If you scan or attach a receipt, the image or document is stored with the related record on your device (and in your private iCloud if sync is on). Text recognition (OCR) is performed **on your device** using Apple's built-in frameworks. **Receipt images are never sent to us or to any third party for processing.**

### d. Location (optional)
If you choose to add a place to an expense, the App uses your device location **once, at that moment**, to look up a readable place name. Location is used only when you request it, is **not** collected in the background, and the resulting place text is stored with that record. You can decline or revoke location access at any time in iOS Settings.

### e. Advertising and device data (Free tier)
When you use the free, ad-supported tier, **Google AdMob** and its ad-technology partners may collect and process certain data to deliver and measure ads, such as a device advertising identifier, IP address, general (coarse) location derived from IP, device type and operating-system version, and ad-interaction events. This processing is performed by Google as an independent controller/processor under its own policies. See Section 7. **If you subscribe, ads and this ad-related processing are removed.**

### f. Diagnostics
We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs and do not operate our own analytics server. Apple may share with us anonymous, aggregated crash and performance statistics **only if** you opt in to sharing diagnostics with developers in iOS Settings; this data is not linked to your identity.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use information to: provide and operate the App's features (recording and displaying your finances, scanning receipts, computing totals and budgets); send the local reminders you enable; sync your data across your devices via iCloud if you turn it on; process Subscriptions through Apple; display and measure advertising on the free tier; maintain security and prevent misuse; and comply with legal obligations. **We do not use your financial information for advertising or profiling.**

## 4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on these legal bases:
- **Performance of a contract** — to provide the App and its features you request.
- **Consent** — for optional features such as location capture, notifications, iCloud sync, and (where required) personalized advertising and the App Tracking Transparency permission. You may withdraw consent at any time.
- **Legitimate interests** — to keep the App secure, to display advertising that funds the free tier, and to improve reliability, balanced against your rights.
- **Legal obligation** — to comply with applicable law.

## 5. Where Your Data Is Stored

By default, your data is stored **only on your device**. If you enable **iCloud sync**, your data is synchronized to your **private iCloud account** using Apple's iCloud/CloudKit service and governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data and cannot view, recover, or restore it. Advertising data (Section 7) is processed by Google on its own infrastructure.

## 6. Notifications

Finovo can send **local notifications** (expense reminders and budget alerts) scheduled on your device. These are off by default and require your permission. We do not send push notifications from a server.

## 7. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free tier is supported by ads served through **Google AdMob**. To suit a personal-finance audience, we configure AdMob to **exclude adult (18+), gambling, and sexually explicit ad content**, though we cannot guarantee specific ads.

- **Data Google may process:** advertising identifier, IP address, coarse location, device and OS information, and ad-interaction data, to serve, cap, and measure ads and to detect fraud.
- **Personalized vs. non-personalized ads & App Tracking Transparency (ATT):** If we enable **personalized** advertising, iOS will present Apple's **App Tracking Transparency** prompt asking your permission to track. If you **deny** (or we use only non-personalized ads), you will be shown **non-personalized/contextual** ads, which use limited data for frequency capping and measurement but not for cross-app tracking. You can change this anytime in **iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking** and **→ Apple Advertising**.
- **Google's policies:** Google's handling of this data is described at https://policies.google.com/privacy and https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. AdMob information is at https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543.
- **Remove ads:** purchase a Subscription.

## 8. How We Share and Disclose Information

We do **not sell your personal information for money.** We share data only as follows:
- **Apple** — for Sign in with Apple, iCloud sync (your private account), and App Store billing for Subscriptions.
- **Google (AdMob)** — advertising data on the free tier, as described in Section 7.
- **Legal and safety** — if required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
- **Business transfer** — if the App or its assets are transferred to another party, subject to this Policy.

> **CCPA/CPRA note:** Using personalized advertising may be considered "sharing" of personal information for "cross-context behavioral advertising" under California law. California residents can opt out by declining the ATT prompt, using the device tracking controls, or subscribing to remove ads. We do not knowingly "sell" personal information as that term is commonly understood.

## 9. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you delete it. **Deleting the Finovo app removes the data stored on that device.** If you enabled iCloud sync, you can remove Finovo's stored data from your iCloud account in **iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud**. Signing out of the App returns you to the login screen without erasing the data on your device. Advertising data is retained by Google according to Google's own retention policies.

## 10. Your Privacy Rights

### GDPR (EU/UK and similar laws)
You have the right to: **access** your personal data; **rectify** inaccurate data; **erase** data ("right to be forgotten"); **restrict** or **object** to processing; **data portability**; and **withdraw consent** at any time. Because your financial data is stored on your own device and iCloud, you can exercise most of these rights directly in the App and in iOS. You also have the right to **lodge a complaint with your local data-protection supervisory authority.**

### CCPA/CPRA (California)
You have the right to: **know** what personal information is collected and how it is used and shared; **delete** personal information; **correct** inaccurate information; **opt out** of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (see Section 8); and **not be discriminated against** for exercising your rights.

To exercise any right, contact **zxapps1993@gmail.com**. We will respond within the time required by applicable law. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.
