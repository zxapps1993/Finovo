## Common questions

**How do I scan a receipt?**
Tap Add, choose Scan, and point the camera at your receipt. Finovo reads it on your device and pre-fills the amount, date, and merchant for you to review before saving.

**Is my data backed up?**
By default your data stays on your device. To back it up and sync across devices, turn on iCloud sync in Settings → Data. It uses your own private iCloud — we never receive a copy.

**How do I remove ads?**
Subscribe to remove ads and unlock premium features.

## Subscriptions

Finovo subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID.

You can cancel or manage your subscription in:

Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
